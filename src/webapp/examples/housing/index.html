Housing vs. Stock Market

This timeplot compares the evolution over time of three indices for the United States since 1975: monetary inflation, house pricing and industrial stock market performance.

Inflation vs. Housing Price Index vs. Dow Jones Industrial Index

Sources: U.S. Department of Labor, Office of Federal Housing Enterprise Oversight (OFHEO) and Dow Jones & Company

The indices have different dimensions and scales but here they have been normalized to better expose relationshis in trends. Move your mouse over the plots to obtain the individual values
